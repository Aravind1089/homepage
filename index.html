👨‍💼 About the Founder

I was born into a humble middle-class family where dreams often took a back seat to responsibilities. But from the very beginning, I knew I was meant to build something—not just for myself, but for others like me who dared to dream beyond their circumstances.

At just 8 years old, I started working at a platform-side idly shop, not because I had to, but because I wanted to ease the weight my parents carried. Among hot plates and morning rush, I learned my first business lesson: never say no to a customer.

That fire to learn and serve stayed with me.
As a teen, I earned a Hardware & Networking certification, repairing PCs, installing OSes, and guiding classmates on what configuration suited their needs—all while pursuing my BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications). I didn’t wait for opportunity—I created it.

By the time I started my MCA, I was running a small video editing business from home, using Movie Maker to craft college ads and symposium highlights. I didn’t know it then, but the seeds of Aegan had already been planted.

After graduation, my career shifted into high gear—diving deep into data platforms, mastering MS Access, VBA, SQL, and Power BI. I’ve spent over 12 years in the tech field, automating 250+ reports, managing 12+ contracts, and even saving 4.5 full-time equivalents (FTEs) through zero-touch BI automation.

And just when things were at their peak, life hit pause—a seizure forced me into heavy medication.
But I refused to give up.

Out of that challenge came clarity and purpose. I built Aegan Analytics & Studio, named after my son—a blend of everything I’ve learned: the empathy from serving idlies, the logic from coding, and the creativity from editing videos.

Today, Aegan isn’t just a business. It’s a story. A mission. A movement.
We transform raw data into insight and videos into emotion.

If you’re a brand looking for a voice, a business craving clarity, or a student ready to learn—Aegan is here for you.
